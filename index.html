Westmorland Cottage Luxury Bed and Breakfast.

Hidden away in the arboretum of the former Moseley Seat this beautiful cottage offers the very best in luxury bed and breakfast.

Caroline & Tim are charming hosts for whom nothing is too much trouble. Tea & cake on arrival in the comfortable beamed lounge or in the attractive gardens.

Delightful bedrooms, with crisp white cotton sheets and soft fluffy towels ensure you are fully pampered. TV, free wifi and tea making facilities in every room. Wake up for an Aga cooked breakfast as well as tucking into home made granola, compote and croissants.

Rolleston on Dove is at the southern tip of the Peak District providing easy access to all that has to offer. Visit the National Arboretum which is close by as well as the brewing museum in Burton on Trent.


Near by

Tutbury Castle, Repton School, Calke Abbey, Catton Hall, Sudbury Hall and Chatsworth are all within easy reach. The village has excellent pubs and restaurants a short walk away.
